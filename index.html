Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new task?

To add a new task, tap the 'Add Task' button on the main screen and fill in the details.

How can I edit a task?

To edit a task, tap on the task you want to edit, make your changes, and save.

How do I receive notifications?

Ensure that notifications are enabled in your app settings and device settings.
